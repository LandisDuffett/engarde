Welcome to

EN GARDE!

To get started, enter your player name and choose your avatar. Then, choose your opponent. When you are ready to start, decide first weather to use a weapon. Use of a weapon takes effort and will cost you health points. Also, if you use a weapon, your opponent will likewise use a weapon in the counterattack. After you attack, your opponent will deal their blow. Continue exchanging blows with your opponent. When one combatant loses all of their health points, the other combatant wins.
