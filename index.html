EN GARDE!

To get started, enter your player name and choose your avatar. Then, choose your opponent. When you are ready to start, choose your attack method to attack your opponent. Choose a weapon before any turn to make your blow more damaging. Use of a weapon takes effort and will cost you 5 health points. Also, if you use a weapon, your opponent will also use a weapon in his counterattack. After you attack, your opponent will deal their blow. Continue exchanging blows with your opponent. When one combatant loses all of their health points, the other combatant wins.
